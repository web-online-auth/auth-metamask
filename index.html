A Crypto Wallet Is A Digital

A crypto wallet is a digital tool that allows users to store, send, and receive cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others. Unlike traditional wallets that hold physical cash, crypto wallets store private keys—secure codes that grant access to a user’s crypto holdings.
